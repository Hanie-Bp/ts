An introduction to tours:

"Fearless Tour" was a concert tour associated with her second studio album, "Fearless," which was released in 2008 . The Fearless Tour marked Swift's first headlining tour and was launched to support the successful album that included hit singles like "Love Story" and "You Belong with Me."

"Speak Now World Tour" was a concert tour by Taylor Swift in support of her third studio album, "Speak Now." The tour took place from February 2011 to March 2012 and covered various cities across North America, Europe, Asia, and Australasia.

"Red Tour" was a concert tour by Taylor Swift in support of her fourth studio album, "Red" which was released in 2012. The tour took place in 2013 and 2014 and marked Swift's third concert tour.

"1989 World Tour" was a highly successful concert tour that accompanied her fifth studio album, "1989," released in 2014. The tour took place in 2015 and marked a departure from her country roots toward a pop-oriented sound.

"Reputation Stadium Tour" took place in 2018. The tour supported her sixth studio album. The tour was known for its elaborate production, special effects, and featured performances of songs from the "Reputation" album, as well as some of her previous hits.
